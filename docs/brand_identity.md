### 1. The Logo Mark: "Unified Focus"
The core of the identity is a minimalist geometric glyph that replaces the previous symmetrical interlocking "W."
* **The Form:** A continuous-line weight design featuring two nested, asymmetric "V" shapes. 
* **The Symbolism:** The lines represent optical paths and data streams that converge on a solid central circle. 
* **The Metaphor:** This circle acts as the "pupil" or "sensor," immediately signaling **focus, detection, and precision**—the three pillars of your work.
* **Utility:** By breaking the perfect symmetry and adding the focal point, we’ve moved away from any resemblance to the Volkswagen logo, creating a mark that is "ownable" in the tech space.

### 2. Typography: "Vita Geometric"
* **Headings:** A clean, geometric sans-serif (Bold). It balances the sharp angles of the logo with open, modern letterforms.
* **Body:** Matching regular weight for high readability in technical documentation and blog posts.
* **Technical UI:** A semi-bold variant for data panels, ensuring that coordinates and "Hamming" distances are legible at a glance.

### 3. Color Palette
The palette is rooted in a "Dark Mode" aesthetic to match the professional, scientific nature of particle physics and computer vision.

| Element | Hex Code | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Background** | `#0B132B` | Deep Navy (The foundation of the UI) |
| **Primary Accent** | `#33C6E3` | Cyan Teal (Used for the logo, targets, and highlights) |
| **Primary Text** | `#E0E1E2` | Light Grey (High contrast for readability) |
| **Muted Data Blue** | `#6A7F93` | For secondary UI elements and data particles |

### 4. Brand Rationale
This identity works because it feels **intentional**. 
* It avoids the "generic corporate" trap by using a singular, modern line-art style.
* It aligns with the "Vite/Vercel/Modern Tech" aesthetic, signaling that your tools are built with a modern stack (Rust, TypeScript, React).
* It scales perfectly from a tiny **16x16 favicon** to a large-scale header without losing its "eye" metaphor.
